About Me

I'm a M.S candidate in physics at Instituto Balseiro. Here I work in the quantum circuits team from the device and sensors group where we are aiming to develop a microwave kinetic inductance detector (MKID) of 16 pixels using granular aluminium.

I received my B.S. in physics from National University of Trujillo in 2020. There, I performed research under Antonio Rivasplata, studying 331 model of particle physics and Quantum Field Theory using Feynman Diagrams and FeynCalc program to compute rate decays for Higgs and gauge bosons. My thesis work can be found at unitru library repository and Concytec. As a result, I had the opportunity to publish my first paper Decay Width of 3-3-1 Model Charged Higgs and Gauge Bosons in Momento Journal. -->

Publications

Decay Width of 3-3-1 model charged Higgs and gauge bosons

Work with Antonio Rivasplata, Guillermo Ramirez and Carlos Rodrigez . Research at TeV energy scales is of great importance for Particle Physics. In this paper we study the 331 model, which was born as an extension of the electroweak symmetry of standard model for the explanation of the three families of quarks and leptons, compute and analyze the decay rates and determine the most likely channels for the decay of Higgs and gauge bosons.

(2022) https://doi.org/10.15446/mo.n64.97711 [Journal link] [OpenReview]

Rate decay of pion - Anchura de decaimiento del pión

Work with Kelman Marin . In this review we study the decay of pion meson, a particle with zero spin. The final state of pion decay can be an electron or a muon. We use Feynamn diagrams to compute its rate decay, and finally, We deduced that the main pion decay channel is through the muon.

(2021) [Journal link] [OpenReview]
